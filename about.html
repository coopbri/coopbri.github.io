Hi there! My name is Brian Cooper. I am a college student, designer, and programmer.

I am currently a computer science and mathematics student at Colorado State University. I am an open-source advocate, and love blockchain technology; I am a big fan of the Ethereum platform in particular. I plan on concentrating my degree on cryptography and blockchain applications, although I am also energetically intrigued by the fields of bioinformatics and computational neuroscience. I believe that the human mind and the universe have the intrinsic ability to, with the right thought patterns, produce a symbiosis that provides a sense of direction. Because of this, I like to hold an open-minded, neuroplastic mindset of wide scope and allow the aforementioned mind-universe relationship to lead the way in my decisions.

From the earliest time my memory provides, I have been insatiably curious. I love learning all sorts of concepts from many different fields because of the remarkable ability it has to subconsciously shape my perspectives of the universe. Throughout my lifetime, I have found myself able to perceive things through many different dynamics thanks to an aggregation of knowledge from domains such as metaphysics, biology, quantum mechanics, cosmology, and philosophy. I read books from a large range of topics and like to stay busy with a course on Udemy or Coursera in my free time.
