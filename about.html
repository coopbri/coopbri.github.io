I am an open-source advocate, and closely follow projects that advocate personal freedom. Some projects that I think are worth checking out are:

  • GNU/Linux: A fantastic, open-source operating system platform ― often shortened to "Linux"
  • Ethereum: A spin on traditional cryptocurrency and blockchain technology, with interactive apps and decentralized contracts in mind
  • Libreboot: Fully open-source personal computer firmware, as a replacement to stock BIOS/UEFI
