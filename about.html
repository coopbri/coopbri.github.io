I am an open-source advocate, and closely follow projects that advocate personal freedom. Some projects that I think are worth checking out are:

  • GNU/Linux: A robust, aggregate operating system platform involving the work of Linus Torvalds, Richard Stallman, and others ― colloquially shortened to "Linux"
  • Ethereum: A spin on traditional cryptocurrency and blockchain technology, with interactive apps and decentralized contracts in mind ― project initialized by Vitalik Buterin and Gavin Wood
  • Libreboot: Fully open-source computer firmware, as a replacement to stock BIOS/UEFI ― project initialized by Leah Rowe

For more privacy-oriented resources, check these out:

  • PrivacyTools: Comprehensive information about privacy-focused providers for everything from DNS resolution services to instant messaging clients
  • PRISM Break: Privacy-focused suggestions for alternatives to common services and applications
  • That One Privacy Site: Detailed reviews of many VPN and email providers
